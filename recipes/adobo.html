Adobo

Descriptions

A Delicious homemade adobo recipe

Ingredients

  • Chicken or Pork
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Bay leaves

Steps

  • Marinate the meat in soy sauce and vinegar for about 30 minutes
  • Heat a pan over medium-high heat and cook the marinated meat until browned on all sides.
  • Add any additional ingredients like garlic, onions, or bay leaves and cook for another 5 minutes.
