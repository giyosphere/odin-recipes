Adobo

Descriptions

A Delicious homemade adobo recipe

Ingredients

  • Pasta Sheets
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce

Steps

  • Boil the pasta sheets until al dente.
  • Layer the pasta, cheese, and tomato sauce in a baking dish.
  • Bake in the oven at 375°F (190°C) for 45 minutes or until bubbly and golden on top.
