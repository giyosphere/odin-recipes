Pancake

Descriptions

A Delicious homemade pancake recipe

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk

Steps

  • Mix the flour, eggs, and milk together until smooth.
  • Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and pour in the pancake batter.
  • Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown on the other side.
